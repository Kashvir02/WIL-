Life Skills

6 Weeks — R750

Introduction

The Life Skills course equips students with essential tools for personal development, communication, and decision-making. It focuses on building confidence, emotional intelligence, and self-awareness.

Modules (StudyBox, 2025)

  • Personal Development and Self-Esteem
  • Effective Communication Skills
  • Stress and Time Management
  • Goal Setting and Decision-Making
  • Financial Literacy and Basic Budgeting

Career Relevance

  • Office administration
  • Customer service
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Management and leadership roles

Conclusion

Life Skills are valuable across all professions, helping individuals thrive personally and professionally while improving teamwork, confidence, and productivity.
