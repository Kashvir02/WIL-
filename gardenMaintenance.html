Garden Maintenance

Introduction

The Garden Maintenance course focuses on plant care, soil health, and general upkeep of outdoor spaces. It’s ideal for those interested in horticulture and property maintenance.

Modules (StudyBox, 2025)

  • Planting and Pruning
  • Soil Management and Fertilization
  • Watering Systems and Maintenance
  • Weed and Pest Control
  • Tool Safety and Use

Career Opportunities

  • Gardener
  • Grounds maintenance worker
  • Landscaping assistant
  • Nursery worker
  • Park maintenance staff

Conclusion

Garden Maintenance provides the skills to care for plants and outdoor environments, supporting both personal gardening and professional landscaping careers.
