Child Minding

6 Weeks — R750

Introduction

This course provides knowledge and skills for those who want to work with young children, focusing on safety, nutrition, and early learning environments.

Modules (StudyBox, 2025)

  • Understanding Child Development
  • Nutrition and Hygiene for Children
  • Creating a Safe and Stimulating Environment
  • First Aid and Emergency Procedures
  • Communication with Parents and Guardians

Career Opportunities

  • Childminder / nanny
  • Daycare assistant
  • Preschool teacher’s aide
  • Au pair
  • Early childhood development assistant

Conclusion

Child Minding is ideal for anyone passionate about early childhood care and education. It opens pathways to work in schools, nurseries, or as an independent caregiver.
