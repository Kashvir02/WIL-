Sewing

6 Weeks — R750

Introduction

Learn how to create and repair garments with a professional finish. The Sewing course introduces practical skills in tailoring, pattern cutting, and garment assembly.

Modules (StudyBox, 2025)

  • Introduction to Sewing Tools and Equipment
  • Understanding Fabrics and Textiles
  • Pattern Design and Cutting
  • Garment Construction and Finishing
  • Alterations and Repairs

Possible Careers

  • Fashion designer
  • Seamstress / tailor
  • Clothing production assistant
  • Textile artist
  • Costume maker

Conclusion

Sewing is both a creative and practical skill that can lead to entrepreneurship and sustainable income generation through clothing repair or custom design services.
