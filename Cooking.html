Cooking

6 Weeks — R750

Introduction

Develop the skills to prepare nutritious, tasty meals. The Cooking course teaches culinary fundamentals, meal planning, and food presentation.

Modules (StudyBox, 2025)

  • Kitchen Safety and Hygiene
  • Food Preparation and Cooking Techniques
  • Nutrition and Meal Planning
  • Cooking for Different Dietary Needs
  • Presentation and Service

Career Opportunities

  • Chef or cook
  • Kitchen assistant
  • Catering business owner
  • Restaurant or café staff
  • Private cook

Conclusion

The Cooking course empowers learners to create healthy, flavorful dishes while opening doors to the food service industry and entrepreneurship.
